I've long wanted to outfit my personal website with a blog, but jekyll setup threw some nasty errors that dissuaded me. Thanks to some web searching, I think I've found a workaround!

I'd like to kick things off by introducing a [report](/Mitochondrial-Richness-Tech-Report-copy.html) that I wrote with my friend and teammate [Oliver Chang](/https://oliverc1623.github.io/) for our final project for Data Mining. We used a dataset of mitochondrial SNV from >2000 tumors spanning 22 tissues. We examined regional heterogeneity, patterns of common variant occurences across different tissues, and used nonparametric methods to estimate frequencies and probabilities of observing previously uncatalogued mitochondrial variants. We promoted our research on several social media outlets.

Thanks for reading and I look forward to sharing updates in research, learning, and whatever else is under the sun! I hope you enjoy!
